How to Join

Being an active member of the ACM offers many benefits, ranging from access to valuable resources to opportunities for growth. Your participation not only enriches your experience but also helps our chapter provide tools and events that support your success. We are diligently working to improve student education and experience—and your involvement is instrumental in this. Membership dues are $20 for one school year. These are optional but help us cover T-shirts, equipment, and snacks.

Steps to join WSC ACM:

  • Create an account at myACM. It’s free and gives you access to ACM resources and opportunities.
  • Send your ACM ID to the President or Vice President so we can officially add you to the chapter roster.
  • Start attending meetings! See our Meeting Times. If you can't make the time, you can still participate in our subcommittees and events.
