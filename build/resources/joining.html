How to Join

Being an active member of the ACM offers many benefits, ranging from access to valuable resources to opportunities for growth. Your participation not only enriches your experience but also helps our chapter give resources and expeience that you can use. We are diligently working towards inproving all student's education and experience, and your involvement is instrumental in this endeavor. Membership dues are $20 for one school year. They are not required but it does help the club afford T-shirts and equipment.

Steps to join WSC ACM:


  • Create an account with myACM: It’s free and gives you access to ACM resources and opportunities.
  • Send your ACM ID to the President or Vice President: This allows us to add you to the WSC ACM chapter, to continue as a club on campus we need at least 20 registered members.
  • Attend our ACM meetings: Check the Meeting Times page for more information. If our meeting times don't work with you, you can still participate in ACM with our subcommittees.
