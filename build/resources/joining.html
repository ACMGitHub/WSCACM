How to Join

Being an active member of the ACM offers many benefits, ranging from access to valuable resources to opportunities for growth. Your participation not only enriches your experience but also helps our chapter give resources and expeience that you can use. We are diligently working towards inproving all student's education and experience, and your involvement is instrumental in this endeavor.

If you are interested in joining ACM you can simply attend one our meetings or talk to anyone that you know is associated with ACM about joining. If our meeting times don't work with you, you can still participate in ACM with our subcommittees.

Membership dues are $20 for one school year. They're are not required but it does help out the club to afford T-shirts and equipment.

Please make a myACM account, it's free and it'll continue supporting our chapter. Use this link to sign up: myACM

Once you have successfully made an account, please forward your ACM ID number to either the President or Secretary. Your contribution is pivotal in helping us achieve our membership goals and further our impact as a chapter.
