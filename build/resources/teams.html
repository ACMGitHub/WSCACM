Microsoft Teams

If you are joining ACM, it is recommended that you download Teams on your computer or phone as a majority of special updates and messages will be presented there. All Wayne State College students have access to Teams through their myWSC account. To get access to the ACM Team, talk to or message an ACM officer to add you.

To view Teams on browser:

  • Log into your myWSC account
  • Go to Resources for Current Students
  • Click on Show More Applications
  • Scroll down until you find Microsoft Teams

To download Microsoft Teams:

  • Go to the Microsoft Teams download page
  • Click Download for desktop (or Download for mobile if needed)
  • Open the downloaded file and follow the installation prompts.
  • Launch Microsoft Teams and sign in with your Wayne State College account
